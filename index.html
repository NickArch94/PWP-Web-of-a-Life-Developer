My Journey and Adventures

Transitioning careers is not something that takes place overnight. I had gone through a lot of failures and rejections. I found myself with a strong desire to learn the ins-and-outs of Fullstack web development. I'm excited to see where this journey takes me and what I will learn along the way.

Having tried my hand at many different aspects of IT, from learning about IT technical roles, to the basics of cybersecurity and the logistical confusions of machine learning, I found my niche in crafting attention-grabbing web applications.

Stepping into my first tech meetup was like walking onto a new kind of set, full of nervous energy and excitement. I quickly discovered a world not of gate-keepers, but of collaborators eager to share their passion. These gatherings became my reminder that we are all scripting our own paths, and it's far more rewarding to do it together.

My path to becoming a web developer has been anything but linear, beginning with many dreams pursued, endless menial job hours worked, and a wishy-washy pursuit of a career in film. I look to chronicle my adventure in pivoting careers, a journey fueled by resources like bountiful YouTube tutorials, The Odin Project, and the obtaining of various CompTIA certifications. This webpage doesn't just document my career-switching journeys, however... In the summer of 2024, life introduced an unexpected plot twist with a Multiple Sclerosis diagnosis. Rather than pausing my ambitions, this moment fueled a deeper desire for connection.
